## 3. Scottish Gaelic Alphabet & Sounds

### Broad vs Slender (Core Concept)

|Type|Vowels|Effect|
|---|---|---|
|**Broad**|a, o, u|Hard consonant|
|**Slender**|e, i|Soft consonant|

**Rule**:

> _Slender vowels surround slender consonants; broad surround broad._

## 13. What Is Scots?

- A **Germanic language**
