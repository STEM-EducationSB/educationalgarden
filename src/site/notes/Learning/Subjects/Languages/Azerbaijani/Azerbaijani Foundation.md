## 1. Identity of Azerbaijani

- **Native name**: Azərbaycan dili
    
- **Language family**: Turkic → Oghuz branch
    
- **Closest relatives**: Turkish, Turkmen
    
- **Primary regions**:
    
    - Azerbaijan
        
    - Iran (South Azerbaijani)
        
    - Russia (Dagestan), Georgia, Turkey
        

There are **two major varieties**:

- **North Azerbaijani** (Republic of Azerbaijan – standard form)
    
- **South Azerbaijani** (Iran – more Persian influence)
    

They are **mutually intelligible**.

---

## 2. Writing System

### Alphabet

Azerbaijani uses a **Latin alphabet** (since 1991):

**32 letters**

`A B C Ç D E Ə F G Ğ H X I İ J K Q L M N O Ö P R S Ş T U Ü V Y Z`

### Special Letters

|Letter|Sound|Approximation|
|---|---|---|
|Ə|/æ/|“a” in _cat_|
|Ç|/tʃ/|ch|
|Ş|/ʃ/|sh|
|Ğ|soft|lengthens vowel|
|X|/χ/|Arabic خ|
|Q|/g/|deep “g”|
|İ|/i/|clear “ee”|
|I|/ɯ/|Turkish ı|
|Ö|/ø/|German ö|
|Ü|/y/|German ü|

Spelling is **highly phonetic**.

---

## 3. Phonology (Sound System)

- **Vowel harmony** (core Turkic feature)
    
- 9 vowels:
    
    - Front: e, ə, i, ö, ü
        
    - Back: a, ı, o, u
        
- Stress usually falls on the **last syllable**
    

---

## 4. Grammar Structure

### Word Order

**SOV** — Subject + Object + Verb

> Mən kitab oxuyuram  
> _I am reading a book_

---

### No Grammatical Gender

Azerbaijani does **not** mark gender.

> o → he / she / it

---

### Agglutinative Language

Meaning is built by **stacking suffixes**:

`ev → evlər → evlərimiz → evlərimizdən house → houses → our houses → from our houses`

---

## 5. Nouns

### Plural

- **-lar / -lər** (vowel harmony)
    

> kitab → kitablar  
> ev → evlər

---

### Cases (6 Main)

|Case|Suffix|Meaning|
|---|---|---|
|Nominative|—|subject|
|Genitive|-ın / -in|of|
|Dative|-a / -ə|to|
|Accusative|-ı / -i|direct object|
|Locative|-da / -də|in|
|Ablative|-dan / -dən|from|

## 7. Verbs

### Infinitive

- Ends in **-maq / -mək**
    

> getmək — to go  
> yazmaq — to write

---

### Present Continuous

**-ır / -ir / -ur / -ür**

> Mən gedirəm — I am going  
> O yazır — He/She is writing

---

### Past Tense

**-dı / -di / -du / -dü**

> Mən getdim — I went

---

### Future Tense

**-acaq / -əcək**

> Mən gedəcəyəm — I will go

---

## 8. Negation

Add **-ma / -mə** before tense:

> Mən getmirəm — I am not going  
> O yazmadı — He/She did not write

---

## 9. Questions

Add **-mı / -mi / -mu / -mü**

> Sən gəlirsənmi? — Are you coming?

---

## 10. Vocabulary Origins

- **Core vocabulary**: Turkic
    
- **Loanwords**: Persian, Arabic, Russian  
    Examples:
    
- kitab (Arabic) — book
    
- məktəb (Arabic) — school
    
- saat (Arabic) — time/hour

## 13. Learning Difficulty (Honest Assessment)

- **Grammar**: Logical, consistent
    
- **Pronunciation**: Easy
    
- **Vocabulary**: Fast growth
    
- **Overall difficulty**: **Easy–Medium**
    

For a structured learner like you, this is a **high-reward language**.
