# German Prepositions

## A. Prepositions with **Dative** (always)

1. **aus** – “ows” – /aʊ̯s/ – out of, from
    
2. **bei** – “bye” – /baɪ̯/ – at, with
    
3. **mit** – “mit” – /mɪt/ – with
    
4. **nach** – “nahkh” – /naːx/ – after, to
    
5. **seit** – “zайт” – /zaɪ̯t/ – since, for
    
6. **von** – “fon” – /fɔn/ – from, of
    
7. **zu** – “tsoo” – /tsuː/ – to
    
8. **gegenüber** – “geh-gen-oo-ber” – /ˈɡeːɡənʔyːbɐ/ – opposite
    
9. **außer** – “ow-ser” – /ˈaʊ̯sɐ/ – except
    
10. **entgegen** – “ent-geh-gen” – /ɛntˈɡeːɡən/ – toward
    

---

## B. Prepositions with **Accusative** (always)

11. **durch** – “doorh” – /dʊʁç/ – through
    
12. **für** – “fюр” – /fyːɐ̯/ – for
    
13. **gegen** – “geh-gen” – /ˈɡeːɡən/ – against
    
14. **ohne** – “oh-neh” – /ˈoːnə/ – without
    
15. **um** – “oom” – /ʊm/ – around
    
16. **bis** – “bis” – /bɪs/ – until
    
17. **entlang** – “ent-lahng” – /ɛntˈlaŋ/ – along
    
18. **wider** – “vee-der” – /ˈviːdɐ/ – against
    
19. **je** – “yeh” – /jeː/ – per
    
20. **pro** – “pro” – /pʁoː/ – per
    

---

## C. **Two-way Prepositions** (Dative = location, Accusative = motion)

21. **an** – “ahn” – /an/ – on, at
    
22. **auf** – “owf” – /aʊ̯f/ – on
    
23. **in** – “in” – /ɪn/ – in
    
24. **über** – “oo-ber” – /ˈyːbɐ/ – over
    
25. **unter** – “oon-ter” – /ˈʊntɐ/ – under
    
26. **vor** – “for” – /foːɐ̯/ – in front of
    
27. **hinter** – “hin-ter” – /ˈhɪntɐ/ – behind
    
28. **neben** – “neh-ben” – /ˈneːbən/ – next to
    
29. **zwischen** – “tsvi-shen” – /ˈtsvɪʃən/ – between
    

---

## D. Prepositions with **Genitive** (formal / written German)

31. **wegen** – “veh-gen” – /ˈveːɡən/ – because of
    
32. **trotz** – “trots” – /tʁɔts/ – despite
    
33. **statt** – “shtat” – /ʃtat/ – instead of
    
34. **anstatt** – “ahn-shtat” – /anˈʃtat/ – instead of
    
35. **außerhalb** – “ow-ser-halp” – /ˈaʊ̯sɐˌhalp/ – outside
    
36. **innerhalb** – “in-er-halp” – /ˈɪnɐˌhalp/ – inside
    
37. **oberhalb** – “oh-ber-halp” – /ˈoːbɐˌhalp/ – above
    
38. **unterhalb** – “oon-ter-halp” – /ˈʊntɐˌhalp/ – below
    
39. **diesseits** – “dees-zайт” – /ˈdiːszaɪ̯ts/ – on this side
    
40. **jenseits** – “yen-zайт” – /ˈjɛnzaɪ̯ts/ – on the other side
