# Norwegian Functional Words

### 1. Conditional Words

_Used to express conditions, hypotheses, or alternatives._

- if – hvis – /vɪs/
    
- unless – med mindre – /med ˈmɛndrə/
    
- else – ellers – /ˈɛlɐs/
    
- otherwise – ellers – /ˈɛlɐs/
    
- whether – om – /ɔm/
    
- provided (that) – forutsatt (at) – /fuːruːˈsat at/
    
- providing (that) – forutsatt (at) – /fuːruːˈsat at/
    
- assuming (that) – under antagelse (av) – /ˈʉndər anˈtɑːɡəlse ɑv/
    
- in case – i tilfelle – /i tɪˈfɛlə/
    
- even if – selv om – /sɛlv ɔm/
    
- only if – bare hvis – /ˈbɑːrə vɪs/
    
- lest – for å unngå – /for o ˈʉŋɡoː/
