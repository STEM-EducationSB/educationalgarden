10. **aallippoq** – “aal-li-pok” – /aːlːipːoq/ – to begin
    
11. **naammassivoq** – “naam-mas-si-vok” – /naːmasːivoq/ – to finish
    
12. **sivippoq** – “si-vi-pok” – /sivipːoq/ – to be long
    
13. **mikivoq** – “mi-ki-vok” – /mikivoq/ – to be small
    
14. **angivoq** – “ang-i-vok” – /aŋivok/ – to be big
    
15. **ukkappoq** – “uk-ka-pok” – /ukːapːoq/ – to be cold
    
16. **kiappoq** – “ki-a-pok” – /kiaːpːoq/ – to be warm
    
17. **nuannaarpoq** – “nu-an-naar-pok” – /nuaŋːaːʁpoq/ – to be happy
