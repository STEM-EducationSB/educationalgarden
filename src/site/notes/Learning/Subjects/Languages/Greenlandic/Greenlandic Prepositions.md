# Greenlandic Prepositions

--------------------------------------------------
1. LOCATION & POSITION
--------------------------------------------------
1. imi – "ee-mee" – /i.mi/ – in, inside
2. sani – "sah-nee" – /sa.ni/ – beside, next to
3. qaanni – "khaah-nee" – /qaːn.ni/ – on, on top of
4. ataani – "ah-tah-nee" – /aː.taː.ni/ – under, beneath
5. saanni – "sah-nee" – /saːn.ni/ – in front of
6. tungaanut – "toong-ah-noot" – /tuŋ.ŋa.nut/ – toward
7. avataani – "ah-vah-tah-nee" – /a.vaː.taː.ni/ – outside
8. iluani – "ee-loo-ah-nee" – /i.lu.a.ni/ – inside of
9. akornani – "ah-kor-nah-nee" – /a.kor.na.ni/ – between
10. ungataani – "oo-ngah-tah-nee" – /u.ŋaː.taː.ni/ – beyond

--------------------------------------------------
2. DIRECTION & MOVEMENT
--------------------------------------------------
11. mut – "moot" – /mut/ – to
12. mit – "meet" – /mit/ – from
13. kut – "koot" – /kut/ – through
14. tikillugu – "tee-kee-loo" – /ti.ki.lu/ – until
15. aqqutigalugu – "ak-koo-tee-loo" – /aq.qu.ti.lu/ – via
16. aqqusilluni – "ak-koo-see-loo" – /aq.qu.si.lu/ – along
17. kaajallallugu – "kah-ya-la-loo" – /kaː.jal.la.lu/ – around
18. utillugu – "oo-tee-loo" – /u.ti.lu/ – back to
19. sammillugu – "sam-mee-loo" – /sam.mi.lu/ – toward (direction)
20. nunnillugu – "noon-nee-loo" – /nun.ni.lu/ – downward toward

--------------------------------------------------
3. TIME & SEQUENCE
--------------------------------------------------
21. siorna – "see-or-nah" – /si.or.na/ – before
22. kingorna – "king-or-nah" – /kiŋ.or.na/ – after
23. ilani – "ee-lah-nee" – /i.la.ni/ – during
24. tamaat – "tah-maht" – /ta.maːt/ – throughout
25. nalaani – "nah-lah-nee" – /na.laː.ni/ – at the time of
26. ullormut – "ool-lor-moot" – /ul.lor.mut/ – per day
27. qaammatip iluani – "kha-mah-tip ee-loo-ah-nee" – /qa.mːa.tip i.lu.a.ni/ – within the month
28. kingusinnerusukkut – "king-goo-see-neh-rook-koot" – /kiŋ.gu.si.ner.suk.kut/ – later
29. siusinnerusukkut – "see-oo-see-neh-rook-koot" – /si.u.si.ner.suk.kut/ – earlier
30. naggataatigut – "nag-gah-tah-tee-gut" – /nag.ga.taː.ti.gut/ – finally

--------------------------------------------------
4. CAUSE, REASON & PURPOSE
--------------------------------------------------
31. pissutigalugu – "pis-see-tee-loo" – /pis.si.ti.lu/ – because of
32. peqqutigalugu – "pek-koo-tee-loo" – /peq.qu.ti.lu/ – due to
33. tunngavigalugu – "toong-ah-vee-gah-loo" – /tuŋ.ŋa.vi.ga.lu/ – based on
34. siunertarlugu – "see-oo-ner-tah-loo" – /si.u.ner.ta.lu/ – in order to
35. taamaattumik – "tah-mah-too-tik" – /taː.maː.tut.tik/ – therefore
36. peqqutaalluni – "pek-koo-tah-loo-nee" – /peq.qu.taːl.lu.ni/ – because
37. tunngavilugu – "toong-ah-vee-loo" – /tuŋ.ŋa.vi.lu/ – on the grounds of
38. tunngavigisarlugu – "toong-ah-vee-sah-loo" – /tuŋ.ŋa.vi.sa.lu/ – relying on
39. pissutaalluni – "pis-soo-tah-loo-nee" – /pis.su.taːl.lu.ni/ – resulting from
40. tunngavigineqarluni – "toong-ah-vee-neh-kah-loo-nee" – /tuŋ.ŋa.vi.ne.qar.lu.ni/ – being grounded in

--------------------------------------------------
