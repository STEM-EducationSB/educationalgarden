# Greenlandic Conjunctions

## 1. Coordinating Conjunctions (Primary)

1. **aamma** – “ahm-ma” – /ˈaːmːa/ – and, also
    
2. **-lu** – “loo” – /lu/ – and (enclitic)
    
3. **imaluunniit** – “ee-ma-loo-ung-goot” – /imɑˈluːŋːut/ – or
    
4. **-veq** – “vek” – /vɛq/ – or (rare, enclitic)
    
5. **kisianni** – “kee-see-an-nee” – /kisiˈanːi/ – but
    
6. **-li** – “lee” – /li/ – but, however (enclitic)
    
7. **taamaattoq** – “taa-maat-tok” – /tɑːˈmɑːtːoq/ – however
    
8. **soorlu** – “soor-loo” – /soːɬu/ – or else
    

---

## 2. Subordinating Conjunctions (Cause, Reason)

9. **pillugu** – “pil-loo” – /ˈpilːu/ – because
    
10. **sunaaffa** – “soo-naf-fa” – /sunɑfːɑ/ – because it turns out
    
11. **taamaammat** – “taa-maam-mat” – /tɑːˈmɑːmːɑt/ – therefore
    
12. **ajunngitsumik** – “a-yoon-gee-mik” – /ɑjunːɡit͡sːik/ – since
    
13. **-mmat** – “mmat” – /mːɑt/ – because (verbal suffix)
    
14. **-gaangami** – “gaang-a-mee” – /ɡɑːŋːɑmi/ – because (temporal-causal)
    

---

## 3. Conditional Conjunctions (If, Unless)

15. **ifølge** – “ee-foe-luh” – /ifølːə/ – if (loan usage)
    
16. **-guni** – “goo-nee” – /ɡuni/ – if
    
17. **-galuarpat** – “ga-loo-ar-pat” – /ɡɑluɑrpɑt/ – even if
    
18. **-pat** – “pat” – /pɑt/ – if, when
    
19. **-galuarpoq** – “ga-loo-ar-pork” – /ɡɑluɑrpɔq/ – although
    

---

## 4. Temporal Conjunctions (Time)

20. **tassa** – “tas-sa” – /tasːɑ/ – when, now that
    
21. **taamani** – “taa-maa-nee” – /tɑːmɑːni/ – at that time
    
22. **ullumi** – “ool-loo-mee” – /ulːumi/ – when today
    
23. **-mat** – “mat” – /mɑt/ – when
    
24. **-gaangat** – “gaang-at” – /ɡɑːŋːɑt/ – whenever
    
25. **siullermik** – “see-ool-lur-mik” – /siulːɛrmik/ – first
    
26. **kingullermik** – “kee-ngool-lur-mik” – /kiŋːulːɛrmik/ – lastly
