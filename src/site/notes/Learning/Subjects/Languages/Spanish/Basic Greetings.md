## **1. Basic Greetings**

These are your everyday, anytime openers.

**Hola**  
– Hello / Hi  
– Universal, works everywhere, any time.

**¿Qué tal?**  
– How’s it going? / What’s up?  
– Very common, friendly, casual.

**¿Cómo estás?**  
– How are you? (informal)

**¿Cómo está usted?**  
– How are you? (formal)

---

## **2. Time-Based Greetings**

Used depending on the time of day.

**Buenos días**  
– Good morning (till around 12–1 pm)

**Buenas tardes**  
– Good afternoon/evening (till sunset)

**Buenas noches**  
– Good night / Good evening (after sunset; also used when saying goodnight)

---

## **3. Responses When Someone Greets You**

You want to sound natural? Use these.

**Bien, gracias. ¿Y tú?**  
– Fine, thanks. And you? (informal)

**Bien, gracias. ¿Y usted?**  
– Fine, thanks. And you? (formal)

**Todo bien.**  
– All good.

**Más o menos.**  
– So-so.

**Muy bien.**  
– Very good.

---

## **4. Casual/Friendly Greetings**

Sounds more natural, used with friends.

**¡Hey!**  
– Hey!

**¡Qué onda!** (Mostly Mexico)  
– What’s up?

**¡Buenas!**  
– Casual “hello,” used any time of day.

---

## **5. Greeting When Meeting Someone**

Polite, used in introductions.

**Mucho gusto.**  
– Nice to meet you.

**Encantado / Encantada.**  
– Pleased to meet you.  
(Encantado if you’re male, Encantada if you’re female.)

---

## **6. Saying Goodbye**

Closely related, and you need these too.

**Adiós**  
– Goodbye

**Chao**  
– Bye (very common)

**Hasta luego**  
– See you later

**Hasta mañana**  
– See you tomorrow

**Nos vemos**  
– See you
