# **Spanish Conjunctions – List of 50**

1. **y** – “ee” – /i/ – and
    
2. **o** – “oh” – /o/ – or
    
3. **pero** – “peh-roh” – /ˈpeɾo/ – but
    
4. **aunque** – “a-oon-keh” – /ˈauŋke/ – although
    
5. **porque** – “por-keh” – /ˈpoɾke/ – because
    
6. **ya que** – “ya keh” – /ˈʝa ke/ – since (because)
    
7. **pues** – “pwes” – /pwes/ – so, well
    
8. **así que** – “ah-see keh” – /aˈsi ke/ – so (therefore)
    
9. **si** – “see” – /si/ – if
    
10. **cuando** – “kwan-doh” – /ˈkwando/ – when
    
11. **mientras** – “myen-tras” – /ˈmjentas/ – while
    
12. **donde** – “don-deh” – /ˈdonde/ – where
    
13. **como** – “koh-moh” – /ˈkomo/ – as, like
    
14. **que** – “keh” – /ke/ – that
    
15. **pero aun así** – “peh-roh a-oon a-see” – /ˈpeɾo aun aˈsi/ – but even so
    
16. **sin embargo** – “seen em-bar-goh” – /sin emˈbaɾɣo/ – however
    
17. **no obstante** – “noh obs-tan-teh” – /no oβsˈtante/ – nevertheless
    
18. **además** – “a-deh-mas” – /aðeˈmas/ – moreover
    
19. **incluso** – “een-klu-soh” – /inˈkluso/ – even (including)
    
20. **ni** – “nee” – /ni/ – nor
    
21. **tanto… como…** – “tan-toh koh-moh” – /ˈtanto ˈkomo/ – both… and…
    
22. **o… o…** – “oh… oh” – /o o/ – either… or…
    
23. **ya… ya…** – “ya… ya” – /ʝa ʝa/ – now… now… (sometimes… sometimes…)
    
24. **porque sí** – “por-keh see” – /ˈpoɾke si/ – just because
    
25. **antes de que** – “an-tes de keh” – /ˈantes de ke/ – before
    
26. **después de que** – “des-pwes de keh” – /desˈpwes de ke/ – after
    
27. **hasta que** – “as-ta keh” – /ˈasta ke/ – until
    
28. **aunque no** – “a-oon-keh noh” – /ˈauŋke no/ – even if not
    
29. **de modo que** – “deh moh-doh keh” – /de ˈmoðo ke/ – so that
    
30. **para que** – “pa-ra keh” – /ˈpaɾa ke/ – so that / in order that
    
31. **a menos que** – “a meh-nos keh” – /a ˈmenos ke/ – unless
    
32. **con tal de que** – “kon tal de keh” – /kon ˈtal de ke/ – provided that
    
33. **siempre que** – “syehm-preh keh” – /ˈsjempɾe ke/ – as long as
    
34. **en cuanto** – “en kwan-toh” – /en ˈkwanto/ – as soon as
    
35. **a la vez que** – “a la vez keh” – /a la βeθ ke/ – at the same time as
    
36. **igual que** – “ee-gwal keh” – /iˈɣwal ke/ – just like
    
37. **así como** – “a-see koh-moh” – /aˈsi ˈkomo/ – just as
    
38. **tal como** – “tal koh-moh” – /ˈtal ˈkomo/ – such as
    
39. **por lo tanto** – “por lo tan-toh” – /poɾ lo ˈtanto/ – therefore
    
40. **por consiguiente** – “por kon-see-gyehn-teh” – /poɾ konsiˈɣjente/ – consequently
    
41. **de hecho** – “deh eh-choh” – /de ˈetʃo/ – in fact
    
42. **o sea** – “oh seh-ah” – /o ˈsea/ – that is / in other words
    
43. **es decir** – “es deh-seer” – /es ðeˈsiɾ/ – that is to say
    
44. **por ejemplo** – “por eh-hem-ploh” – /poɾ eˈxemplo/ – for example
    
45. **al contrario** – “al kon-trah-ryoh” – /al konˈtɾaɾjo/ – on the contrary
    
46. **más bien** – “mas byen” – /mas ˈbjen/ – rather
    
47. **en cambio** – “en kam-byoh” – /en ˈkambjo/ – instead / on the other hand
    
48. **así pues** – “ah-see pwes” – /aˈsi pwes/ – thus
    
49. **por más que** – “por mas keh” – /poɾ mas ke/ – no matter how much
    
50. **puesto que** – “pwes-toh keh” – /ˈpwest̪o ke/ – since (given that)
