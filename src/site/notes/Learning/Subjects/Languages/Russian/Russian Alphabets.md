# **How To Memorize The Entire Russian Alphabet (Fast, Reliable, Permanent)**

I’m giving you a method that matches how your brain actually stores language patterns.

---

## **1. Group by “looks like English” vs “tricks”**

### **A. Letters that look like English AND sound like English (easy 9)**

A, E, K, M, O, T — same sound  
Plus:  
С = S  
Р = R  
Н = N   
B = V  
Y = U  

You already know **11 letters instantly**.

---

## **2. Letters that LOOK familiar but LIE (False Friends)**

These are your danger zone. Master these early.

|Russian|Looks like…|Actually sounds like…|
|---|---|---|
|В|B|V|
|Н|H|N|
|Р|P|R|
|С|C|S|
|У|Y|U|
|Х|X|KH (like German ch)|

Memorization trick:  
**“In Russian, letters PRETEND to be your friend, then punch you.”**

Say this line 3 times and the “false friend” idea sticks permanently.

---

## **4. Learn vowels in pairs (Russian trick for instant reading)**

Russian vowels come in two versions:

### **Hard set:**

А, О, У, Ы, Э

### **Soft set (with “y/ya/ye/yo/yu”):**

Я, Ё, Ю, Е, И

Memorize it as:

**Hard = no Y. Soft = has Y-sound.**

This alone makes you read 70% of Russian accurately.
