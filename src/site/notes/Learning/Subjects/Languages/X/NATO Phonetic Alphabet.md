## 1. NATO Phonetic Alphabet (Alpha–Bravo–Charlie)

This is a **spelling alphabet**, used to **spell words clearly over radio, phone, aviation, military, police, and emergency communication**.

### Full NATO Phonetic Alphabet

|Letter|Code Word|
|---|---|
|A|Alpha|
|B|Bravo|
|C|Charlie|
|D|Delta|
|E|Echo|
|F|Foxtrot|
|G|Golf|
|H|Hotel|
|I|India|
|J|Juliett|
|K|Kilo|
|L|Lima|
|M|Mike|
|N|November|
|O|Oscar|
|P|Papa|
|Q|Quebec|
|R|Romeo|
|S|Sierra|
|T|Tango|
|U|Uniform|
|V|Victor|
|W|Whiskey|
|X|X-ray|
|Y|Yankee|
|Z|Zulu|

### Example

- “CAT” → **Charlie Alpha Tango**
    
- “INDIA” → **India November Delta India Alpha**
    

**Purpose:** eliminate confusion between similar-sounding letters (B/P/D, M/N, etc.).
