# **1) What is Maithili? (Foundation Context)**

**Definition & Identity**  
Maithili is a **New Indo-Aryan language** primarily spoken in the _Mithila region_ of India (notably Bihar) and Nepal. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maithili_language?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

**Distribution & Status**

- Officially recognized in the **Eighth Schedule of the Indian Constitution**; it’s widely used in education and administration in parts of India. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maithili_language?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
    
- It has a rich literary tradition dating back centuries. [www.biharscert.in](https://scert.bihar.gov.in/public/uploads/deled_books/S-9-G_%E0%A4%AE%E0%A5%88%E0%A4%A5%E0%A4%B2%E0%A5%80_%281%29.pdf?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
    

**Why It Matters**  
Maithili is not only a communicative language but also a **cultural beacon** — crucial for literature, folk arts, music, and identity in Mithila.

## **4) 20 Simple Maithili Sentences**

Here’s common usable content with English meaning:

1. **हम घर जा रहल छी।** — I am going home.
    
2. **अपना नाम की छि?** — What is your name?
    
3. **ई घर सुंदर अछि।** — This house is beautiful.
    
4. **हमरा पानी चाही।** — I want water.
    
5. **तूँ किताब पढ़ रहल छें।** — You are reading a book.
    
6. **ओ खाय रहल अछि।** — He/She is eating.
    
7. **हम बजार जा रहल छी।** — I am going to the market.
    
8. **कुत्ता दौड़ैत अछि।** — The dog is running.
    
9. **भात गरम अछि।** — The rice is hot.
    
10. **हम स्कूल जाइ छी।** — I go to school.
    
11. **अहाँ कोना छी?** — How are you?
    
12. **हम नीक छी।** — I am fine.
    
13. **तूँ कहिया अबअएब?** — When will you come?
    
14. **ओ कल्हि आबथि।** — He/She will come tomorrow.
    
15. **हम पढ़लहुँ।** — I studied.
    
16. **ओ खेल रहल छै।** — He/She is playing.
    
17. **हमरा किताब दे।** — Give me the book.
    
18. **ई बढियाँ भोजन अछि।** — This is good food.
    
19. **हम सोच रहल छी।** — I am thinking.
    
20. **हमरा ऐहिठाम सहायता चाही।** — I need help here.

## **5) Tenses Mini-Primer (Present, Past, Future)**

Maithili expresses tense partly through verb inflection:

**Present**

- _हम जाए रहल छी_ — I am going.
    

**Past**

- _हम गेलहुँ_ — I went.
    

**Future**

- _हम जएब_ — I will go.
    

(_Conjugation varies with person and politeness level; but these are the simplest patterns._) [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maithili_grammar?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

## **6) Similarity With Bengali and Hindi**

### **Vocabulary**

- Many basic Maithili words look like Hindi and sometimes Bengali — _घर (ghar)_, _पानी (pani)_, _स्कूल (school)_. [Learn Entry](https://www.learnentry.com/english-maithili/1000-most-common-maithili-words/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
    

### **Grammar Patterns**

- All three languages commonly use **Subject–Object–Verb (SOV)** order. [MustGo.com](https://www.mustgo.com/worldlanguages/maithili/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
    

### **Pronouns**

- **हम** in Maithili is like **हम** in Hindi and **আমি (ami)** in Bengali (first person).
    
- **अहाँ** (formal you) is close to Hindi **आप**, and Bengali **আপনি (apni)** — all polite forms.
    

### **Gender & Inflection**

- Hindi and Maithili mark nouns and verbs for gender and number; Bengali doesn’t mark gender in the same way. [travelwithlanguages.com](https://travelwithlanguages.com/blog/bengali-and-hindi.html?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
    

### **Scripts**

- Maithili historically used **Tirhuta** script, distinct but related to Bengali script family. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tirhuta_script?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
    

### **Influence**

- All are part of the **Indo-Aryan language family**, so core vocabulary and grammar have shared heritage.
