# French Functional Words
## 1. Conditional Words

1. **si** – si – /si/ – if
    
2. **à moins que** – a mwen kə – /a mwɛ̃ kə/ – unless
    
3. **sinon** – si-non – /si.nɔ̃/ – else / otherwise
    
4. **autrement** – o-tʁə-mɑ̃ – /otʁə.mɑ̃/ – otherwise
    
5. **que ce soit** – kə sə swa – /kə sə swa/ – whether
    
6. **à condition que** – a kɔ̃.di.sjɔ̃ kə – /a kɔ̃.di.sjɔ̃ kə/ – provided (that)
    
7. **pourvu que** – puʁ.vy kə – /puʁ.vy kə/ – providing (that)
    
8. **en supposant que** – ɑ̃ sy.po.zɑ̃ kə – /ɑ̃ sy.po.zɑ̃ kə/ – assuming (that)
    
9. **au cas où** – o ka u – /o ka u/ – in case
    
10. **même si** – mɛm si – /mɛm si/ – even if
    
11. **seulement si** – sə.lə.mɑ̃ si – /sə.lə.mɑ̃ si/ – only if
    
12. **de peur que** – də pœʁ kə – /də pœʁ kə/ – lest

## 10. Modal Verbs (Functional Equivalents)

French does not have true modals like English; it uses **semi-modals**.

1. **pouvoir** – pu.vwaʁ – /pu.vwaʁ/ – can / could
    
2. **pourrait** – pu.ʁɛ – /pu.ʁɛ/ – might
    
3. **devoir** – də.vwaʁ – /də.vwaʁ/ – must / should
    
4. **falloir** – fa.lwaʁ – /fa.lwaʁ/ – must (impersonal)
    
5. **vouloir** – vu.lwaʁ – /vu.lwaʁ/ – will / would
    
6. **être censé** – ɛtʁ sɑ̃.se – /ɛtʁ sɑ̃.se/ – ought (to)
