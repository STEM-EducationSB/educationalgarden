## French Prepositions

1. **à** – “ah” – /a/ – to, at, in
    
2. **chez** – “shey” – /ʃe/ – at the home of, with
    
3. **dans** – “dahn” – /dɑ̃/ – in, inside
    
4. **en** – “ahn” – /ɑ̃/ – in, to, into
    
5. **sur** – “sewr” – /syʁ/ – on, upon
    
6. **sous** – “soo” – /su/ – under, beneath
    
7. **devant** – “duh-vahn” – /dəvɑ̃/ – in front of
    
8. **derrière** – “deh-ree-ehr” – /dɛʁjɛʁ/ – behind
    
9. **entre** – “ahn-tr” – /ɑ̃tʁ/ – between
    
10. **par** – “par” – /paʁ/ – by, through
    
11. **pour** – “poor” – /puʁ/ – for, in order to
    
12. **avec** – “ah-vek” – /avɛk/ – with
    
13. **sans** – “sahn” – /sɑ̃/ – without
    
14. **de** – “duh” – /də/ – of, from
    
15. **du** – “dy” – /dy/ – of the, from the (masc.)
    
16. **des** – “day” – /de/ – of the, from the (pl.)
    
17. **au** – “oh” – /o/ – at the, to the (masc.)
    
18. **aux** – “oh” – /o/ – at the, to the (pl.)
    
19. **à côté de** – “ah koh-tay duh” – /a kote də/ – next to, beside
    
20. **loin de** – “lwan duh” – /lwɛ̃ də/ – far from
    
21. **près de** – “preh duh” – /pʁɛ də/ – near, close to
    
22. **en face de** – “ahn fas duh” – /ɑ̃ fas də/ – facing, opposite
    
23. **au-dessus de** – “oh duh-suh duh” – /o dy.su də/ – above
    
24. **au-dessous de** – “oh duh-suh duh” – /o də.su də/ – below
    
25. **derrière** – “deh-ree-ehr” – /dɛʁjɛʁ/ – behind
    
26. **devant** – “duh-vahn” – /dəvɑ̃/ – in front of
    
27. **pendant** – “pahn-dahn” – /pɑ̃dɑ̃/ – during, for (time)
    
28. **depuis** – “duh-pwee” – /dəpɥi/ – since, for (time)
    
29. **jusqu’à** – “zhoos-kah” – /ʒyska/ – until, up to
    
30. **avant** – “ah-vahn” – /avɑ̃/ – before
    
31. **après** – “ah-prey” – /apʁɛ/ – after
    
32. **chez moi** – “shey mwa” – /ʃe mwa/ – at my place
    
33. **chez toi** – “shey twah” – /ʃe twa/ – at your place
    
34. **entre… et…** – “ahn-tr… eh…” – /ɑ̃tʁ… e/ – between… and…
    
35. **parmi** – “par-mee” – /paʁmi/ – among
    
36. **selon** – “suh-lohn” – /s(ə)lɔ̃/ – according to
    
37. **malgré** – “mal-greh” – /malɡʁe/ – despite
    
38. **grâce à** – “grahs ah” – /ɡʁɑs a/ – thanks to, due to
    
39. **envers** – “ahn-vehr” – /ɑ̃vɛʁ/ – toward, regarding
    
40. **vers** – “vehr” – /vɛʁ/ – toward, about
    
41. **auprès de** – “oh-prey duh” – /o pʁɛ də/ – near, close to
    
42. **au lieu de** – “oh lyeuh duh” – /o ljø də/ – instead of
    
43. **dans le but de** – “dahn luh byoo duh” – /dɑ̃ lə by də/ – with the aim of
    
44. **en raison de** – “ahn ray-zohn duh” – /ɑ̃ ʁɛzɔ̃ də/ – because of
    
45. **par rapport à** – “par rah-por ah” – /pa ʁapɔʁ a/ – in relation to
    
46. **à propos de** – “ah proh-poh duh” – /a pʁɔpo də/ – about, concerning
    
47. **d’après** – “dah-prey” – /dapʁɛ/ – according to
    
48. **au sujet de** – “oh soo-zhay duh” – /o syʒɛ də/ – regarding
    
49. **en dépit de** – “ahn day-pee duh” – /ɑ̃ depi də/ – in spite of
    
50. **grâce à** – “grahs ah” – /ɡʁɑs a/ – thanks to
