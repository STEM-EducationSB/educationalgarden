## French Conjunctions

- **et** – “ay” – /e/ – and
    
- **ou** – “oo” – /u/ – or
    
- **mais** – “meh” – /mɛ/ – but
    
- **donc** – “dohnk” – /dɔ̃k/ – therefore, so
    
- **car** – “kar” – /kaʁ/ – for, because
    
- **ni** – “nee” – /ni/ – neither, nor
    
- **or** – “or” – /ɔʁ/ – now, yet (literary)
    
- **si** – “see” – /si/ – if
    
- **que** – “kuh” – /kə/ – that
    
- **lorsque** – “lor-sk” – /lɔʁsk/ – when
    
- **quand** – “kahn” – /kɑ̃/ – when
    
- **comme** – “komm” – /kɔm/ – as, since
    
- **puisque** – “pweessk” – /pɥisk/ – since, because
    
- **parce que** – “parss kuh” – /paʁs kə/ – because
    
- **afin que** – “ah-fan kuh” – /afɛ̃ kə/ – so that, in order that
    
- **bien que** – “byan kuh” – /bjɛ̃ kə/ – although
    
- **quoique** – “kwoh-uhk” – /kwak/ – although
    
- **tandis que** – “tahn-dees kuh” – /tɑ̃di kə/ – while
    
- **pendant que** – “pahn-dahn kuh” – /pɑ̃dɑ̃ kə/ – while
    
- **alors que** – “ah-lor kuh” – /alɔʁ kə/ – whereas
    
- **si bien que** – “see byan kuh” – /si bjɛ̃ kə/ – so that
    
- **de sorte que** – “duh sort kuh” – /də sɔʁt kə/ – so that
    
- **de manière que** – “duh ma-nyair kuh” – /də man.jɛʁ kə/ – so that
    
- **à condition que** – “ah kon-dee-syon kuh” – /a kɔ̃disjɔ̃ kə/ – provided that
    
- **à moins que** – “ah mwahn kuh” – /a mwɛ̃ kə/ – unless
    
- **en même temps que** – “ahn mem tahn kuh” – /ɑ̃ mɛm tɑ̃ kə/ – at the same time as
    
- **dès que** – “day kuh” – /dɛ kə/ – as soon as
    
- **aussitôt que** – “oh-see-toh kuh” – /osis.to kə/ – as soon as
    
- **avant que** – “ah-vahn kuh” – /avɑ̃ kə/ – before
    
- **après que** – “ah-pray kuh” – /apʁɛ kə/ – after
    
- **si… alors** – “see… ah-lor” – /si alɔʁ/ – if… then
    
- **non seulement… mais aussi** – “non suh-luh-mahn… meh oh-see” – /nɔ̃ sɔləmɑ̃ mɛ osi/ – not only… but also
    
- **soit… soit…** – “swah… swah” – /swa swa/ – either… or…
    
- **ni… ni…** – “nee… nee” – /ni ni/ – neither… nor…
    
- **ou bien… ou bien…** – “oo byan… oo byan” – /u bjɛ̃ u bjɛ̃/ – either… or…
    
- **car** – “kar” – /kaʁ/ – for, because
    
- **en effet** – “ahn eh-feh” – /ɑ̃n efɛ/ – indeed, in fact
    
- **par conséquent** – “par kon-seh-kahn” – /paʁ kɔ̃sekɑ̃/ – consequently
    
- **c’est pourquoi** – “say par-kwah” – /sɛ puʁkwa/ – that’s why
    
- **ainsi** – “ahn-see” – /ɛ̃si/ – thus, therefore
    
- **donc** – “dohnk” – /dɔ̃k/ – so
    
- **ou bien** – “oo byan” – /u bjɛ̃/ – or else
    
- **même si** – “mem see” – /mɛm si/ – even if
    
- **autant que** – “oh-tahn kuh” – /otɑ̃ kə/ – as much as, as far as
    
- **bien que** – “byan kuh” – /bjɛ̃ kə/ – although
    
- **quoi que** – “kwah kuh” – /kwa kə/ – whatever, although
    
- **pendant que** – “pahn-dahn kuh” – /pɑ̃dɑ̃ kə/ – while
    
- **tandis que** – “tahn-dees kuh” – /tɑ̃di kə/ – whereas
    
- **alors que** – “ah-lor kuh” – /alɔʁ kə/ – whereas
    
- **comme si** – “komm see” – /kɔm si/ – as if
