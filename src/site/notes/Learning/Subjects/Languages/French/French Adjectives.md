## French Adjectives

- **grand** – “grahn” – /ɡʁɑ̃/ – big, tall
    
- **petit** – “puh-tee” – /pəti/ – small, little
    
- **jeune** – “zhuhn” – /ʒœn/ – young
    
- **vieux** – “vyuh” – /vjø/ – old
    
- **nouveau** – “noo-voh” – /nuvo/ – new
    
- **beau** – “boh” – /bo/ – beautiful, handsome
    
- **joli** – “zho-lee” – /ʒoli/ – pretty, nice
    
- **mauvais** – “mo-veh” – /movɛ/ – bad
    
- **bon** – “bohn” – /bɔ̃/ – good
    
- **gentil** – “zhon-tee” – /ʒɑ̃ti/ – kind, nice
    
- **méchant** – “meh-shahn” – /meʃɑ̃/ – mean, nasty
    
- **fort** – “for” – /fɔʁ/ – strong
    
- **faible** – “febl” – /fɛbl/ – weak
    
- **rapide** – “rah-peed” – /ʁapid/ – fast
    
- **lent** – “lahn” – /lɑ̃/ – slow
    
- **heureux** – “uh-ruh” – /øʁø/ – happy
    
- **triste** – “treest” – /tʁist/ – sad
    
- **riche** – “reesh” – /ʁiʃ/ – rich
    
- **pauvre** – “poh-vr” – /povʁ/ – poor
    
- **intelligent** – “an-tel-ee-zhahn” – /ɛ̃tɛliʒɑ̃/ – intelligent
    
- **stupide** – “stoo-peed” – /stypid/ – stupid
    
- **chaud** – “sho” – /ʃo/ – hot
    
- **froid** – “frwah” – /fʁwa/ – cold
    
- **long** – “lohn” – /lɔ̃/ – long
    
- **court** – “koor” – /kuʁ/ – short
    
- **large** – “larzh” – /laʁʒ/ – wide, broad
    
- **étroit** – “ay-trwah” – /etʁwa/ – narrow
    
- **propre** – “pro-pr” – /pʁɔpʁ/ – clean
    
- **sale** – “sahl” – /sal/ – dirty
    
- **facile** – “fa-seel” – /fasil/ – easy
    
- **difficile** – “dee-fee-seel” – /difikil/ – difficult
    
- **cher** – “sher” – /ʃɛʁ/ – expensive
    
- **bon marché** – “bohn mar-shay” – /bɔ̃ maʁʃe/ – cheap
    
- **important** – “an-por-tahnt” – /ɛ̃pɔʁtɑ̃/ – important
    
- **intéressant** – “an-teh-reh-sahn” – /ɛ̃teʁesɑ̃/ – interesting
    
- **ennuyeux** – “on-wee-uh” – /ɑ̃nɥijø/ – boring
    
- **content** – “kon-tahn” – /kɔ̃tɑ̃/ – happy, pleased
    
- **mécontent** – “meh-kon-tahn” – /mekɔ̃tɑ̃/ – unhappy
    
- **facile à comprendre** – “fa-seel ah kon-pron-dre” – /fasil a kɔ̃pʁɑ̃dʁ/ – easy to understand
    
- **difficile à comprendre** – “dee-fee-seel ah kon-pron-dre” – /difikil a kɔ̃pʁɑ̃dʁ/ – hard to understand
    
- **beau/belle** – “boh / bell” – /bo/ /bɛl/ – beautiful
    
- **laid** – “leh” – /lɛ/ – ugly
    
- **blanc** – “blahn” – /blɑ̃/ – white
    
- **noir** – “nwar” – /nwaʁ/ – black
    
- **rouge** – “roozh” – /ʁuʒ/ – red
    
- **bleu** – “bluh” – /blø/ – blue
    
- **vert** – “vehr” – /vɛʁ/ – green
    
- **jaune** – “zhon” – /ʒon/ – yellow
    
- **orange** – “oh-rahng” – /ɔʁɑ̃ʒ/ – orange
    
- **rose** – “rohz” – /ʁoz/ – pink
    
- **violet** – “vee-oh-lay” – /vjɔlɛ/ – purple
    
- **marron** – “mah-rohn” – /maʁɔ̃/ – brown
    
- **gris** – “gree” – /ɡʁi/ – gray
    
- **beau garçon** – “boh gar-sohn” – /bo ɡaʁsɔ̃/ – handsome boy
    
- **jolie fille** – “zho-lee fee” – /ʒoli fij/ – pretty girl
    
- **grand homme** – “grahn om” – /ɡʁɑ̃ nɔm/ – tall man
    
- **petite femme** – “puh-teet fam” – /pəti fam/ – small woman
    
- **jeune garçon** – “zhuhn gar-sohn” – /ʒœn ɡaʁsɔ̃/ – young boy

- **grand chien** – “grahn shee-en” – /ɡʁɑ̃ ʃjɛ̃/ – big dog

- **joli chat** – “zho-lee shah” – /ʒoli ʃa/ – pretty cat
