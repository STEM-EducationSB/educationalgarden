# Italian Conjunction

## **1. Coordinating Conjunctions (Congiunzioni Coordinanti)**

### **A. Copulative (Addition)**

1. **e** – “eh” – /e/ – and
    
2. **anche** – “AHN-keh” – /ˈaŋ.ke/ – also
    
3. **pure** – “POO-reh” – /ˈpu.re/ – also / too
    
4. **né** – “neh” – /ne/ – nor
    
5. **né…né** – “neh…neh” – /ne ne/ – neither…nor
    

---

### **B. Disjunctive (Choice / Alternative)**

6. **o** – “oh” – /o/ – or
    
7. **oppure** – “op-POO-reh” – /opˈpu.re/ – or else
    
8. **ovvero** – “ov-VEH-roh” – /ovˈve.ro/ – or rather
    
9. **altrimenti** – “al-tree-MEN-tee” – /altriˈmen.ti/ – otherwise
    

---

### **C. Adversative (Contrast)**

10. **ma** – “mah” – /ma/ – but
    
11. **però** – “peh-ROH” – /peˈrɔ/ – however
    
12. **tuttavia** – “toot-ta-VEE-ah” – /tut.taˈvi.a/ – nevertheless
    
13. **eppure** – “ep-POO-reh” – /epˈpu.re/ – yet
    
14. **bensì** – “ben-SEE” – /benˈsi/ – rather
    

---

### **D. Conclusive (Result)**

15. **dunque** – “DOON-keh” – /ˈduŋ.kwe/ – therefore
    
16. **quindi** – “KWEEN-dee” – /ˈkwin.di/ – so
    
17. **perciò** – “per-CHO” – /perˈtʃɔ/ – thus
    
18. **pertanto** – “per-TAN-toh” – /perˈtan.to/ – hence
    

---

## **2. Subordinating Conjunctions (Congiunzioni Subordinanti)**

### **A. Causal (Cause)**

19. **perché** – “per-KEH” – /perˈke/ – because
    
20. **poiché** – “poy-KEH” – /poiˈke/ – since
    
21. **siccome** – “see-KOH-meh” – /siˈkom.me/ – since
    
22. **dato che** – “DAH-toh keh” – /ˈda.to ke/ – given that
    

---

### **B. Final (Purpose)**

23. **affinché** – “af-fin-KEH” – /affinˈke/ – so that
    
24. **perché** – “per-KEH” – /perˈke/ – in order that
    
25. **allo scopo di** – “AL-loh SKOH-poh dee” – /ˈal.lo ˈskɔ.po di/ – with the aim of
    

---

### **C. Temporal (Time)**

26. **quando** – “KWAN-doh” – /ˈkwan.do/ – when
    
27. **mentre** – “MEN-treh” – /ˈmen.tre/ – while
    
28. **finché** – “feen-KEH” – /finˈke/ – until
    
29. **appena** – “ah-PEH-nah” – /apˈpe.na/ – as soon as
    
30. **dopo che** – “DOH-poh keh” – /ˈdo.po ke/ – after
    

---

### **D. Conditional**

31. **se** – “seh” – /se/ – if
    
32. **qualora** – “kwa-LOH-rah” – /kwaˈlo.ra/ – if ever
    
33. **purché** – “poor-KEH” – /purˈke/ – provided that
    
34. **a condizione che** – “ah kon-dee-TSEE-oh-neh keh” – /a konditˈtsjo.ne ke/ – on condition that
    

---

### **E. Concessive (Despite)**

35. **benché** – “ben-KEH” – /benˈke/ – although
    
36. **sebbene** – “seb-BEH-neh” – /sebˈbe.ne/ – even though
    
37. **nonostante** – “non-oh-STAN-teh” – /nonoˈstan.te/ – despite
    
38. **anche se** – “AN-keh seh” – /ˈaŋ.ke se/ – even if
    

---

### **F. Comparative**

39. **come** – “KOH-meh” – /ˈko.me/ – as
    
40. **piuttosto che** – “pyoot-TOH-stoh keh” – /pjutˈto.sto ke/ – rather than
    
41. **così come** – “ko-ZEE KOH-meh” – /koˈzi ˈko.me/ – just as
    

---

### **G. Consecutive (Result / Effect)**

42. **così che** – “ko-ZEE keh” – /koˈzi ke/ – so that
    
43. **talmente…che** – “tal-MEN-teh…keh” – /talˈmen.te ke/ – so much that
    

---

## **3. Correlative Conjunctions**

44. **sia…sia** – “SEE-ah…SEE-ah” – /ˈsi.a ˈsi.a/ – both…and
    
45. **non solo…ma anche** – “non SOH-loh…mah AN-keh” – /non ˈso.lo ma ˈaŋ.ke/ – not only…but also
    
46. **o…o** – “oh…oh” – /o o/ – either…or
    
47. **né…né** – “neh…neh” – /ne ne/ – neither…nor
    

---

## **4. Explanatory / Clarifying**

48. **cioè** – “cho-EH” – /tʃoˈɛ/ – that is
    
49. **infatti** – “in-FAT-tee” – /inˈfat.ti/ – in fact
    
50. **ossia** – “OH-see-ah” – /ˈos.sja/ – namely
    

---
