# Italian Prepositions

## I. Preposizioni Semplici (Simple Prepositions) — 9

1. **a** – “ah” – /a/ – to, at
    
2. **da** – “dah” – /da/ – from, by
    
3. **di** – “dee” – /di/ – of, from
    
4. **in** – “een” – /in/ – in, into
    
5. **con** – “kon” – /kon/ – with
    
6. **su** – “soo” – /su/ – on, about
    
7. **per** – “pehr” – /pɛr/ – for, through
    
8. **tra** – “trah” – /tra/ – between, among
    
9. **fra** – “frah” – /fra/ – between, among
