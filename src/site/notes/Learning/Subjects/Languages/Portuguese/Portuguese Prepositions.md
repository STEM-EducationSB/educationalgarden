## 1. Basic / Core Prepositions (20)

1. **a** – “ah” – /a/ – to, at
    
2. **de** – “dee” – /dʒi/ (BR) /də/ (PT) – of, from
    
3. **em** – “eng” – /ẽj̃/ – in, on, at
    
4. **por** – “por” – /poʁ/ – by, through
    
5. **para** – “pa-ra” – /ˈpa.ɾa/ – to, for
    
6. **com** – “kong” – /kõ/ – with
    
7. **sem** – “seng” – /sẽj̃/ – without
    
8. **sob** – “sob” – /sɔb/ – under
    
9. **sobre** – “so-bree” – /ˈsɔ.bɾi/ – about, over
    
10. **até** – “ah-teh” – /aˈtɛ/ – until
    
11. **desde** – “dez-dee” – /ˈdez.dʒi/ – since, from
    
12. **entre** – “en-tree” – /ˈẽ.tɾi/ – between
    
13. **contra** – “kon-tra” – /ˈkõ.tɾa/ – against
    
14. **após** – “ah-posh” – /aˈpɔs/ – after
    
15. **antes de** – “an-tes dee” – /ˈɐ̃.tʃis dʒi/ – before
    
16. **perante** – “pe-ran-tee” – /peˈɾɐ̃.tʃi/ – before, in front of
    
17. **durante** – “doo-ran-tee” – /duˈɾɐ̃.tʃi/ – during
    
18. **semelhante a** – “se-me-lyan-che ah” – /se.meˈʎɐ̃.tʃi a/ – similar to
    
19. **salvo** – “sal-voo” – /ˈsal.vu/ – except
    
20. **exceto** – “eks-se-to” – /ekˈsɛ.tu/ – except

38. **à direita de** – “ah dee-rei-ta dee” – /a dʒiˈɾej.tɐ dʒi/ – to the right of
