## Important Technical Notes (Worth Knowing)

- **Sigma has two lowercase forms**:
    
    - **σ** → used at the beginning or middle of a word
        
    - **ς** → used at the end of a word
        
- Greek letters are foundational in:
    
    - **Mathematics** (π, Σ, Δ, θ)
        
    - **Physics** (λ, Ω, μ)
        
    - **Engineering & Computing**
        
    - **Astronomy and Cosmology**
        
- Greek has **no letter beyond Omega**—Omega literally means _“great O / end”_.
